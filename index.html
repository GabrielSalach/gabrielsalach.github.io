About Me

Passionate self-taught game developper, I grew up dreaming about all the crazy adventures I wanted to create, and it's my goal to make this dream a reality. Amour of Ghost

Made during the 2022 University of Montpellier Imagine GameJam. You play as a kid who needs to find their way to their bed. Unfortunately, their house has turned into a maze and is now haunted by ghosts ! Each time a ghost follows you, your fear meter increases rapidly and once it completes, you lose ! Furtunately, you can close your eyes, which makes the fear meter fill up slowly, you won't be able to see where you're going though so be careful !
